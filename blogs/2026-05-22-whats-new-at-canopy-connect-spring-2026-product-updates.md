Canopy Connect released three updates to its insurance data intake platform: clients can now select their preferred method for sharing insurance information (connecting accounts, uploading files, completing forms, or phone contact), the Policy Paste extension now handles more form field types and includes a keyboard shortcut, and multi-page document uploads can be grouped together as a single submission.
