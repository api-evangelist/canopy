DecSight, Canopy Connect's document intelligence technology, now extracts intelligence from insurance documents and images. The update expands accepted document types beyond declaration pages to include notices and endorsements, supports image file formats alongside PDFs, and enables multi-image grouping for better context preservation when clients submit policy documents as photo series.
