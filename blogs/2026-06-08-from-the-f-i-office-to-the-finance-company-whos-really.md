The article examines the accountability gap in auto lending where insurance verification falls between dealership F&I offices and finance companies. It explains how "the buyer said yes" differs from verified active coverage, describes the risks when borrowers lack compliant insurance, and advocates for real-time carrier-verified data at the point of sale to close this verification gap.
